About SoumiTattva

SoumiTattva is dedicated to supporting the mental well-being of students, providing valuable resources and tools designed to help manage exam stress and promote overall mental health. We understand the unique pressures students face and strive to offer a confidential and reliable platform where they can find support and guidance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and accessible space for students to address their mental health needs, particularly concerning exam-related stress. We leverage advanced AI technologies to proactively identify students who may be at risk of depression or suicidal tendencies, enabling timely intervention and support. Furthermore, we provide personalized AI-driven study schedules tailored to individual learning styles and needs, aiming to optimize learning efficiency and reduce stress. We are committed to fostering a supportive environment where students feel empowered to prioritize their mental well-being alongside their academic pursuits.
